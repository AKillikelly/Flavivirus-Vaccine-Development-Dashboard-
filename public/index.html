Curation methodology

Automation is designed to be conservative: structured records can add clinical signals, but official or manually configured source rows are required for authorization and programme claims.

Stage assignment

Rows map to the highest documented target-specific milestone, not to generic platform maturity. For example, licensed yellow fever 17D vaccines are post-licensure for YFV, while a Zika mRNA candidate remains clinical-stage until a target-specific authorization signal appears.

Automated refresh

scripts/fetch_pipeline.py pulls ClinicalTrials.gov v2 records, deduplicates by NCT ID, classifies target/candidate/platform with deterministic aliases, merges with source-backed seed rows, and writes the CSV plus an audit report.

Status caveat

Regulatory status, trial recruitment, and country policy can change quickly. Use the row-level source URLs and generated audit report before making operational, investment, travel-health, or procurement decisions.
